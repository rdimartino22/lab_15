  ralphthecorgi - Happy 10th birthday, Georgie! I never could have imagined that we would have to fight so hard for Georgie to see this day, but we made it. You made it. And you are hungry, playful, and yourself this morning, which makes today all the more perfect. ♥️ We celebrated our beautiful boy with a truly perfect visit to Tahoe on Monday, where Georgie delighted in the first snow of the season, and we will continue celebrating him today, and every day.

Although George is very ill, he wakes up in a warm home and is loved and spoiled every single day. Sadly, many dogs are not so lucky. They give their best years to their owners only to be cast aside and given up when health issues or old age come knocking. So to celebrate George’s birthday, we are raising money for @queensbeststumpydogrescue, a wonderful corgi rescue dedicated to saving corgs who have been surrendered or abandoned due to medical or behavioral issues. If you are interested and able, you can make a donation for Georgie’s birthday!
